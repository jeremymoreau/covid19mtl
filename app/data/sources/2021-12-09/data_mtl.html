Changes in the situation in Montréal

Montréal’s regional public health department has published a portrait of the COVID-19 situation on the Island of Montréal. The portrait provides data on the number of cases and deaths in each Montréal borough and linked city.

Cases are presented by age group and type of setting (community or CHSLD, residences or housing resources), as well as for healthcare workers. Deaths are shown by type of setting.

The data will be updated every Wednesday.

  • See the situation on the island of Montréal, December 7, 2021 (PDF) (Only in French)

    Note: Individualized reports are not produced for boroughs or linked cities with fewer than 50 cases.

Report on the current outbreaks on the island of Montreal

This weekly report examines the current outbreaks on the island of Montreal. It includes a map of the active outbreaks in Montreal by type of establishment and the number of cases associated with the outbreak. The report also illustrates the evolution of the number of outbreaks by type. In addition, the reader will find a section that highlights the key numbers and compares them to the report from the previous week. 

  • See the report on the current outbreaks on the island of Montreal, December 7, 2021 (PDF) (Only in French)
